Our Vision
  • We develop, organize, provide access to and preserve materials to meet the needs of present and future generations of students and scholars. We explore and implement innovative technologies and services to deliver information and scholarly resources conveniently to users anytime/anyplace. We also provide well-equipped and functional physical spaces where students can pursue independent learning and discovery outside the classroom.

Our Mission
  • Our Supreme Library will strengthen and enhance the quality of education, research and service. The Library promote intellectual growth and creativity by developing collections, facilitating access to information resources, teaching the effective use of information resources and critical evaluation skills with help of books and videos

Our Values
  • We recognize members as our primary focus
  • We promote equity of access to information
  • We respect user privacy
  • We support self-education, learning, research
